Training Module

You are massively underestimating this. Trained operators protect your reputation. Untrained ones destroy it.

Onboarding Structure (60–90 Minutes)

Every new operator must complete all three modules before being permitted to conduct an unsupervised survey. This is non-negotiable.

MODULE 1 System Orientation 20–30 min
  • Mission overview — what MeasurePRO does and what it doesn't do (15 min lecture, Doc 01)
  • Hardware identification — name and purpose of each component
  • Data flow walkthrough — how a measurement becomes a deliverable
  • Safety rules — power sequence, no survey under RTK-free GPS, amplitude validation
  • Assessment: trainee must correctly identify all hardware components and state the startup order from memory
MODULE 2 Supervised Practical 30–40 min
  • Trainee completes the Pre-Deployment Checklist (Doc 04) independently — trainer watches without prompting
  • Trainee connects all hardware in correct startup order
  • Trainee runs ground reference calibration and calibration validation pass
  • Trainee conducts a short survey of 10 known structures — trainer evaluates POI capture quality, photo composition, and type selection
  • Trainee exports deliverable and identifies any data gaps
  • Assessment: Trainer signs off on each step. Any step failed must be repeated the next day.
MODULE 3 Edge Cases & Recovery 15–20 min
  • Trainer simulates 5 faults from the Troubleshooting Matrix (Doc 09) — trainee must diagnose and resolve
  • Required scenarios: GPS loss mid-survey, laser constant --, wrong protocol, app crash, LiDAR service disconnect
  • Trainee must complete QC Checklist (Doc 08) on the practical survey data
  • Assessment: All 5 scenarios resolved without assistance

Certification Levels

MeasurePRO Operator Certification

Three tiers. Operators must re-certify annually or after any major system update.

Level 1 — Certified Operator

Can conduct standard vertical clearance surveys unsupervised. Can use USB/Bluetooth GPS. Cannot configure GNSS or laser protocols.

Requires: Modules 1–3 completed and signed off by certified trainer.

Level 2 — Senior Operator

Can configure all hardware, calibrate ground reference, use Duro GNSS/IMU, export engineering-grade deliverables, operate multi-laser systems.

Requires: Level 1 + 20 supervised surveys + written assessment on Doc 07 deliverables scope.

Level 3 — System Administrator

Can configure vehicle profiles, manage LiDAR companion service, train new operators, sign off QC checklists, authorise deliverables.

Requires: Level 2 + direct nomination by organisation principal.

Certified Trainer

Delivers Modules 1–3, issues Level 1 certifications. Must hold Level 2 minimum and have conducted >50 surveys.

Why Certification Matters for Your Business

  • Authority — clients trust a certified-operator system over "we trained them on the job"
  • Liability — when something goes wrong on-site, certification documents that the operator was trained to a defined standard
  • Recurring revenue — annual recertification creates a natural touchpoint for training fees and system updates
  • Lock-in — operators trained on MeasurePRO are less likely to switch to a competitor system mid-career
  • Support cost reduction — the Pre-Deployment Checklist and Troubleshooting Matrix alone eliminate 30–50% of support calls from untrained operators
