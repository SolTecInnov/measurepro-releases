Mission-Level Overview

Operational clarity — not marketing. Read this first.

MeasurePRO is a mobile-first field measurement platform that captures vertical clearances, lateral widths, road profiles, and GPS geometry in real time — turning hours of manual survey work into a single drive pass.

What Problems It Eliminates

Manual tape measurement

Traditional clearance surveys require stopping at every overhead obstacle, deploying a measuring pole, and recording by hand. MeasurePRO captures clearance continuously at road speed.

Post-survey data entry errors

Handwritten notes get lost, mis-keyed, or lack GPS coordinates. Every POI in MeasurePRO is geo-tagged, timestamped, and photo-documented the moment it is captured.

Disconnected equipment

Laser, GNSS receiver, cameras, and LiDAR all feed a single unified interface. No separate loggers, no separate software, no merging spreadsheets after the fact.

Report production time

Exporting a complete route deliverable (CSV, GeoJSON, Shapefile, DXF, LandXML) takes seconds from inside the app. No post-processing software required for standard deliverables.
