Deliverables Definition

Be explicit. Vague deliverables create disputes. Clear deliverables build trust.

Use this page as a contract reference. Share it with clients before engagement. Scope creep starts when deliverables are undefined. Amend the "NOT included" section per project as needed.

What the Client Gets — Standard Deliverable

Clearance Profile (CSV)

Every recorded POI with: unique ID, GPS coordinates (lat/lon), measurement date/time, clearance height in metres, POI type (bridge/overpass/wire/powerLine etc.), operator notes, and photo filename references. Format: CSV, importable into Excel with no modification.

Route GeoJSON

GPS track as a LineString feature. All POIs as Point features with full properties. Suitable for direct import into any GIS platform (QGIS, ArcGIS, Mapbox). CRS: WGS84 by default; alternate CRS on request.

Survey ZIP Package

Complete self-contained archive: all export formats, all captured photos (JPEG), metadata.json (calibration provenance, app version, operator, survey date, ground reference value). This file can be re-imported into MeasurePRO for independent verification.

Road Profile (if GNSS Recording was enabled)

Chainage-based profile with: chainage (m), GPS coordinates, altitude, grade (%), K-factor, cross-slope (°), curve radius (m). Flagged alert segments (banking >threshold, curve <minimum radius). Formats: CSV + LandXML or DXF (Civil 3D / AutoCAD compatible).

What Is NOT Included

Structural engineering assessment

Clearance measurements tell you the geometric clearance at time of survey. They do not assess structural capacity, load rating, or fitness for specific vehicle types. A structural engineer must make those determinations.

Permit applications

Data is provided to support a permit application. MeasurePRO survey data does not constitute a permit, approval, or road authority sign-off.

Interpretation of results

The CSV and GeoJSON files contain measured data. Interpretation, risk assessment, and recommendations are outside scope unless a consulting engagement is separately contracted.

Real-time route safety certification

MeasurePRO records conditions at time of survey. Road conditions change (new obstacles, maintenance, seasonal sag on overhead wires). The client is responsible for validating current conditions before each load movement.

Indoor or off-road environments

GPS accuracy degrades or fails entirely in tunnels, under dense canopy, and in buildings. Survey data from these sections should be treated with caution and noted as approximate.
