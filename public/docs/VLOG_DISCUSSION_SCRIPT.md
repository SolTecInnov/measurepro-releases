## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Setting Options

**Option A: Inside Vehicle (Preferred)**
- Natural environment for product
- Can demonstrate features live
- Good audio control
- Weather-independent

**Option B: Job Site Outdoors**
- More dynamic visuals
- Can show full vehicle setup
- May need wind protection for audio
- Golden hour lighting ideal

### Camera Setup

**Primary Camera:**
- Medium shot, both subjects in frame
- Eye-level or slightly above
- Rule of thirds composition

**Secondary Camera (B-Roll):**
- Close-ups of tablet/screen
- Insert shots of features being demonstrated
- Cutaways to equipment

**Audio:**
- Lavalier mics on both participants (wireless)
- Backup recorder
- Monitor for quality

### Lighting

**If Interior (Vehicle):**
- Soft fill light from camera direction
- Balance with natural light from windows
- Avoid harsh shadows

**If Exterior:**
- Shoot during golden hour (soft, warm light)
- Use reflector for fill
- Diffusion if midday (avoid harsh sun)

### Editing Notes

**Pace:**
- Keep it moving, but not rushed
- Natural pauses for emphasis
- Cut out long silences, "ums," false starts

**Inserts:**
- Add screen captures when discussing features
- B-roll of equipment when mentioned
- Graphics for pricing (clean overlays)

**Lower Thirds:**
- Names and titles on first appearance
- Website/contact info at end
- Social media handles

**Music:**
- Light background music (low volume)
- Upbeat but not distracting
- Fade under when demonstrating features
