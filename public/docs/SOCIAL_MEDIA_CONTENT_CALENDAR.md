## CONTENT STRATEGY OVERVIEW

### Goals:
1. **Awareness:** Introduce MeasurePRO to transportation professionals
2. **Education:** Demonstrate value and use cases
3. **Engagement:** Build community and trust
4. **Conversion:** Drive free trials and demo requests

### Target Audience:
- Oversized load operators
- Fleet managers
- Transportation companies
- Survey and engineering firms
- Construction equipment transport

### Content Pillars:
1. **Educational** (40%) - How-tos, tips, tutorials
2. **Product Features** (30%) - Demos, feature highlights
3. **Customer Stories** (15%) - Testimonials, case studies
4. **Industry Insights** (10%) - News, trends, regulations
5. **Company Culture** (5%) - Behind-the-scenes, team

### Posting Frequency:
- **LinkedIn:** 5x/week (M-F)
- **Twitter/X:** Daily
- **Facebook:** 3x/week
- **Instagram:** 4x/week (3 feed + 4 stories)
- **YouTube:** 2x/month (longer content)

---

## PLATFORM-SPECIFIC GUIDELINES

### LinkedIn (Professional B2B Focus)
**Best Times:** Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM, 12 PM
**Content Types:** Industry insights, case studies, feature demos, webinars
**Tone:** Professional, authoritative, educational
**Max Length:** 1,300 characters (shorter for better engagement)

### Twitter/X (Real-Time Engagement)
**Best Times:** Monday-Friday, 9 AM, 12 PM, 5 PM
**Content Types:** Quick tips, news, GIFs, polls, thread stories
**Tone:** Conversational, timely, direct
**Max Length:** 280 characters (use threads for longer content)

### Facebook (Community Building)
**Best Times:** Wednesday-Friday, 1-3 PM
**Content Types:** Videos, customer stories, community posts, events
**Tone:** Friendly, approachable, community-focused
**Max Length:** 80 characters for max engagement (link in comments)

### Instagram (Visual Storytelling)
**Best Times:** Monday, Wednesday, Friday 11 AM-1 PM
**Content Types:** Photos, Reels, Stories, carousel posts
**Tone:** Visual, inspirational, behind-the-scenes
**Feed:** High-quality images/videos
**Stories:** Casual, authentic, daily updates

### YouTube (Long-Form Education)
**Best Times:** Friday-Sunday, 12-3 PM
**Content Types:** Tutorials, demos, customer interviews, webinars
**Tone:** Informative, helpful, professional
**Length:** 5-15 minutes (optimize for watch time)
